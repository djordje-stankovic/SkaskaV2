Commitment

Scroll

The real tradition is not about historical facts nor customs but all about true values passed on.

Where

Produced in the heart of Serbia, called Shumadia and famous for an authentic plum brandy, widely acclaimed for the mellowness of its landscapes and its people.

What & How

Plum brandy distilled by honoring a tradition that underlines the culture of welcoming wayfarer with kindness and bonhomie.

Why

There are so much pride and dignity in our people's way to honor new friendships with carefully distilled plum brandy, permeated with the lore of true values: honesty and devotion. We take pride in following this culture in the process of distilling "Skaska".
